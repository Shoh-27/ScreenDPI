What to Include in Your Message

To help us assist you more effectively, please include:

  • Subject Line: A brief description of your inquiry
  • Device Information: Your operating system and browser (if technical issue)
  • Screen Details: Your screen resolution and size (if relevant)
  • Description: A clear explanation of your question or issue
  • Screenshots: If reporting a bug, screenshots are very helpful

Response Time

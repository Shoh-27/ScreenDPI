Understanding Smartphone PPI

Most modern smartphones exceed 400 PPI, well beyond the threshold of human perception at typical viewing distances (10-12 inches). The benefits of ultra-high PPI include:

  • Extremely sharp text rendering
  • Smooth curved edges and diagonals
  • High-quality VR readiness (phones used in headsets)
  • Marketing differentiation

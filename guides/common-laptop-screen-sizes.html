Common Laptop Screen Sizes

Reference guide to laptop display sizes, resolutions, and pixel density

13-Inch Laptops

Resolution PPI Examples
1920 × 1080 ~170 PPI Budget ultrabooks
2560 × 1600 ~227 PPI MacBook Air M2/M3
2880 × 1800 ~254 PPI MacBook Pro 13"

14-15 Inch Laptops

Size Resolution PPI Category
14" 1920 × 1080 ~157 PPI Standard business laptop
15.6" 1920 × 1080 ~141 PPI Budget laptop
15.6" 3840 × 2160 ~282 PPI Premium 4K laptop
16" 3456 × 2234 ~254 PPI MacBook Pro 16"

17-Inch Laptops

Larger laptops for gaming and workstation use:

  • 1920 × 1080: ~130 PPI (standard gaming)
  • 2560 × 1440: ~173 PPI (high-end gaming)
  • 3840 × 2160: ~260 PPI (4K workstation)
